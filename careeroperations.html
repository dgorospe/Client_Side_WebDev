Careers - Operations:

The Flight Operations department manages various duties of the operational side within the Interplanetary Travel Group.

Main duties include:

  • Training and scheduling of Flight Deck Crew to ensure that they meet all licensing and regulatory requirements and can execute high-quality operation in a safe, efficient and customer-friendly manner.
  • The navigation and technical sections are in charge of effective surveying of all new routes and making available all navigation, performance and other necessary operational data to flight crew, to ensure safe air and ground operations.
  • Investigating, monitoring and disseminating information on all accidents, incidents and other occurrences involving Interplanetary Travel aircraft come within the scope of this function.
