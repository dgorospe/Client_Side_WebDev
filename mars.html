Mars:

The next destination on the available to fly to is Mars, which is 34.8 million miles away. Mars is roughly a half days flight, just like on the Moon, Mars also has a place for everyone to stay at with roughly the same commodities and options. A small city has also been made on the planet due to the artificial atmosphere and oxygen.

The city houses people who either farm on the planet with the excavated water resources found on the planet or with people who maintain the resort. On Mars you are able to take a rover out in groups and explore the vast terrain and witness the many beautiful sites safely. We also have areas in which you can experience the low gravity, roam around a bit, and float around so you can get that full outer space feel to the trip. A fun way in which we use the low gravity is with low gravity tennis games on the resort area. These games are fun for all who come and allow four people to play at a time.
