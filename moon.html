Moon:

The first destination which you can choose from is the Moon, which is 238,900 miles away. With our new advanced spaceships the moon is only a 3 hour flight away. Just because it’s the closest doesn’t mean it is lacking from the other destination spots because the moon has its own perks of going to it.

On the Moon there is a resort built there so that your stay is comfortable and relaxing. You will of course need to pay separate to stay at this resort but it is worth every dollar. The resort also has a penthouse area for all those one-way ticket holders that you can rent out and stay in for as long as you pay for it. The resort has everything from a pool, a tour which takes you around to see some of the planet, and many other things.

Also located on the planet is an amusement park which has a vast majority of rides which are themed to match the moon setting. The amusement park comes complimentary with your trip to the Moon. The many rides and roller-coaster also offer you a better view of the planet by taking you high up into the air letting you see all that you can see and enjoy your time.
