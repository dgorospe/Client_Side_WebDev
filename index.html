Mission Statement

At Interplanetary Travel, our mission is to provide the highest quality interplanetary travel experience to each one of our clients. Whether it is a quick weekend getaway to the gorgeous crater-filled plains of the Moon, or a long family vacation to the jaw-dropping beautiful Rings of Saturn, our friendly staff and expert pilots will ensure the most comfortable ride possible. At Interplanetary Travel, our clients' satisfaction defines who we are, and we aim to continue providing the top of the line travel experience in this solar system. Who We Are

Our company was founded on the idea that traveling doesn't have to be earthbound. In 2005, Divina and Plamen launched SpaceInn Inc., the first orbital satellite hotel over earth. Since that time, they have begun working with Raul and Richard, founders of spaceline.com. In 2010 the group formed the world's first full-service space travel company, Interplanetary Travel. With over 50 years of combined experience in the aerospace and aeronautics industries, our team provides travelers with a unparalleled space travel experience.
