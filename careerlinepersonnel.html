Careers - Line Personnel:

Line Personnels are an essential part of our team. They perform various tasks in operations and customer related services. Providing world class customer service to customers from various different countries is their top priority while maintaining the company's interest. Below is a wide-range of positions which you may apply for.

Spacecraft Pilot:

A spacecraft pilot's job is to safely fly the spacecraft. Vast knowledge of computer skils to maneuver and troubleshoot the spacecraft is a must. Spacecraft pilots must be certified and accredited.

Main duties include:

  • Checking and logging flights.
  • Monitoring the spacecraft systems and reporting data.
  • Communicating with the FAA
  • Working extended hours and possibilty of inconsistent shift.

Flight attendants:

Main duties include:

  • Making sure passengers are safe.
  • Providing great customer service.
  • Greeting passengers and seating them, ensuring that they are properly situated prior to take-off.
  • INstructing passngers on emergency procedures.
  • Helping passengers in the event of an emergency is the most important responsibility of a flight attendant.

Gate agent:

Main duties include:

  • Verifying names on passenger manifest, separating portions of passenger's ticket and stamping or marking tickets.
  • Directing passengers to air-terminal facilities.
  • Opening gates and allowing passengers to board the airplane.

Reservation clerks:

Main duties include:

  • Processing all reservation requests from customers.
  • Providing clerical support to the reservation staff.
  • Maintaining and managing a logbook for reservation activities.
  • Instructing and communicating to guests, visitors, and customers on corporate policies and regulations.

Security guards:

Main duties include:

  • Operating X ray equipment (full training will be given)
  • Conducting searches on passengers, luggage, vehicles, people detained by HM immigration and random searches on airport staff
  • Confiscating prohibited items
  • Securing the facility acting as a visual deterrent as well as reporting any damages.
