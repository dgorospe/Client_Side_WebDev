Careers - Sales and Marketing:

At Interplanetary Travel, we offer highly competitive sales and marketing careers. Sales and Marketing are two different disciplines. Our sales team books new business, and the marketing team creates strategies for product development, pricing, promotions and distribution.

Sales Management

Main duties include:

  • Developing annual sales strategies for a company.
  • Setting sales goals, determining which products to develop based on customer satisfaction and profit margin.
  • Supervising and assigning employee activities.
  • Providing critical employee data which is used in determining employee compensation levels, commission and bonus structures.

Marketing Management

Main duties include:

  • Aiding the sales team by creating ongoing activities that promote the company and its products.
  • Gathering critical data from customers
  • Keeping abreast of current trends by attending conventions, trade shows, and conferences.
  • Working with in-house staff or contractors to help develop any advertising, promotions, social media and public relations activities necessary.
